Privacy Policy

Privacy, kept deliberately narrow

This policy explains what TAME Driftly stores on your device, which permissions may be requested, and how those permissions affect app features.

本政策说明 TAME Driftly 会在本机保存哪些信息、可能请求哪些权限，以及这些权限会如何影响应用功能。

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Information stored on device

Local only

TAME Driftly stores a limited set of local preferences on the current device, including onboarding status and selected reminder options. This version does not create a TAME Driftly account or maintain a personal cloud profile.

TAME Driftly 仅在当前设备上保存少量本地偏好信息，例如是否完成引导页以及你选择的提醒设置。本版本不会创建 TAME Driftly 账户，也不会维护个人云端资料。

Notifications

Optional permission

If you enable bedtime reminders, TAME Driftly requests notification permission so the app can deliver a local reminder at the time you choose.

如果你开启了睡前提醒，TAME Driftly 会请求通知权限，以便在你设定的时间发送本地提醒。

Tomorrow Outlook

No location required

Tomorrow Outlook in this version is generated on device from TAME Driftly's built-in nightly themes. It does not request location access and does not fetch live weather data.

本版本中的“明日建议”会基于 TAME Driftly 内置的夜间主题在设备端生成，不会请求定位权限，也不会获取实时天气数据。

Managing permissions

User control

You can turn off notification access at any time in iPhone Settings. If notification permission is withdrawn, bedtime reminders will stop working until you enable it again.

你可以随时在 iPhone 设置中关闭通知权限。通知权限被关闭后，睡前提醒会暂停，直到你重新授予权限为止。

Data not requested by this version

Not collected

This version of TAME Driftly does not request access to camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or Health data.

本版本 TAME Driftly 不会请求相机、麦克风、通讯录、照片或健康数据权限。

Purchases and subscriptions

Apple billing

Subscriptions and billing are handled by Apple through In-App Purchase. TAME Driftly reads Apple purchase entitlement status only to unlock or restore premium access on the current device.

订阅与计费由 Apple 通过 App 内购买处理。TAME Driftly 仅会读取 Apple 返回的购买权益状态，用于在当前设备上解锁或恢复高级会员权限。

Data retention and deletion

On-device lifecycle

Local preferences remain on your device until you change them, remove the app, or clear related permissions and reminder settings. Because this version does not maintain a TAME Driftly account database, removing the app also removes its local data from that device.

本地偏好会保留在你的设备上，直到你主动修改、删除应用，或清除相关权限与提醒设置。由于本版本没有独立的 TAME Driftly 账户数据库，删除应用也会一并移除该设备上的本地数据。

Policy changes

Updates

This policy may be updated over time. Continued use of TAME Driftly after a policy update means the revised version applies to your use of the app.

本政策可能会随着版本更新而调整。若你在政策更新后继续使用 TAME Driftly，即表示你同意适用更新后的内容。
