How To Use

A simple rhythm for bedtime

TAME Driftly is designed to stay light: begin with one feature, keep the routine calm, and return whenever the evening needs to slow down.

TAME Driftly 的使用逻辑很简单：先从一个入口开始，让节奏保持轻松安静，在夜晚需要慢下来的时候再回来使用即可。

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Get started

Step 1

Open the app, complete onboarding, and start from the Tonight tab for the fastest path into a bedtime routine.

打开应用并完成引导后，优先从 Tonight 页面开始，这是进入睡前节奏最快的入口。

Play a sound

Step 2

Open the library, choose a sound, and start playback. You can pause, resume, and set a sleep timer from the sound detail view.

进入声音库，选择一个音景开始播放。你可以在声音详情页暂停、继续播放，或设置定时关闭。

Use the breathing coach

Step 3

Open the Breathe tab and begin a short guided session. The pacing guide is designed to support a slower, calmer evening rhythm.

打开 Breathe 页面并开始一段短时呼吸引导。节奏设计偏向更慢、更柔和的夜间放松状态。

Set a bedtime reminder

Step 4

In Profile, turn on reminders and choose a time. TAME Driftly will send a local notification if notification permission has been granted.

在 Profile 页面开启提醒并选择时间。如果已经授予通知权限，TAME Driftly 会按设定发送本地提醒。

Use Tomorrow Outlook

Step 5

Open Tonight to view Tomorrow Outlook. In this version, the card builds a lightweight next-day suggestion from TAME Driftly's built-in nightly themes without requesting location or live weather access.

打开 Tonight 页面即可查看“明日建议”。本版本会基于 TAME Driftly 内置的夜间主题生成轻量推荐，不会请求定位权限，也不会访问实时天气数据。

Access premium content

Step 6

Open Premium from the library or paywall. If you already subscribe, use Restore Purchases to recover access on the current device.

你可以从声音库或订阅页进入 Premium。如果已经订阅，可使用 Restore Purchases 在当前设备上恢复会员权限。
